Welcome to Webringie!

Your hub for enhancing SEO visibility through connected metadata.

What is Webringie?

Webringie is an innovative tool designed to boost your website's visibility in search engines by linking metadata between websites with similar topics.

How to Get Started

To integrate Webringie into your site:

  1. Create a metadata.json file and configure it correctly.
  2. Include the following script tag in your HTML:
    3. <script src="https://juliusthejules.github.io/webringie/js/webringie.js" defer></script>
  3. Explore our demo page for a hands-on experience!
